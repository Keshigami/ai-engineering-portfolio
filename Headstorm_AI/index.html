About HeadstormAI

We’re expanding our AI Implementation practice with a hands-on Computer Vision Engineer who will lead model training and orchestration efforts for document and blueprint understanding.

About the Role

This is a senior individual contributor role suited for someone who thrives in applied research-to-production environments. You will architect, train, and optimize computer vision systems that interpret complex architectural plans — enabling automated annotation, layout recognition, and semantic understanding of technical drawings.

You’ll work within a cross-functional AI delivery team (data engineers, strategists, consultants) to transform unstructured images into structured intelligence. You’ll also mentor junior engineers, contribute to reusable IP, and drive model performance from prototype to production.

Requirement: Applicants must provide a portfolio, GitHub, or demonstrable work samples. Submissions without evidence of prior applied computer vision work will not be considered.

Key Responsibilities

Model Design & Training

Develop and fine-tune computer vision and OCR models for blueprint and document analysis.

Image Processing Pipelines

Implement contour detection, edge extraction, vectorization, segmentation, and geometric normalization workflows.

Multimodal & Vision-Language AI

Integrate state-of-the-art models like Florence, CLIP, SAM2, Kosmos, and GroundingDINO into multimodal pipelines.

Architecture Innovation

Dataset Creation

Pipeline Orchestration

Cross-Functional Collaboration

Thought Leadership
